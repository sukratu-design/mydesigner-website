FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

How does billing work?

Monthly plans are billed on the date you subscribe and renew automatically each month. The Daily plan is billed per day with a minimum commitment of 3 days. The Yearly plan is billed once upfront for the full year. You can cancel monthly plans at any time before your next billing cycle.

What happens to unused requests?

There are no "unused requests" to worry about. MyDesigner operates as an unlimited subscription — you can submit as many requests as you like during your billing period. Requests are worked on based on your plan's concurrency (1, 2, or 3 at a time). There is nothing to roll over.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, the price difference is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. When you downgrade, the new price takes effect at your next billing date. Contact us and we will handle the switch immediately.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No. There are zero setup fees, no onboarding charges, and no hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay. Source files, revisions, and project management access are all included in every plan.
