FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

How does billing work?

Ongoing creative support is billed monthly and can be adjusted before the next billing cycle. Focused sprints are scoped and billed as projects, with the exact payment schedule confirmed before work begins.

How do you prioritize the work?

We keep an active creative queue and prioritize the highest-impact work each cycle. Sprints are scoped around one clear outcome, while ongoing support keeps the creative system moving month after month.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can move between a focused sprint, ongoing monthly rhythm, or deeper Creative Growth Partner setup as your needs change. Contact us and we will recommend the cleanest path.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No. There are zero setup fees, no onboarding charges, and no hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay. Source files, revisions, and project management access are all included in every plan.
