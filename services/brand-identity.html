FAQ

Brand Identity — Questions Answered

Is this only logo design?

No. Logos can be included, but the goal is a usable brand system that helps every future asset stay consistent.

Can you refresh an existing brand?

Yes. A refresh can modernize the identity while preserving the recognition and equity that should not change.

Do you create brand guidelines?

Yes. Guidelines can include practical rules, examples, templates, and AI-ready prompts where appropriate.

How does this help with AI content creation?

A clear brand system gives AI tools constraints, examples, and rules so output gets closer to your brand instead of becoming generic.
