How this work runs

A shipping rhythm with human taste.

  1. 1. Diagnose the creative bottleneck.
  2. 2. Gather the context that affects quality.
  3. 3. Create the first direction quickly.
  4. 4. Review with human taste and business judgment.
  5. 5. Ship the asset and store the learning in Client Memory.

Client Memory

Every engagement builds Client Memory.

Your brand rules, examples, reusable patterns, past decisions, prompts, templates, and performance learnings should not disappear after each request. MyDesigner turns that context into a living creative system so every new asset starts faster and stays closer to your brand.

Client Memory should store the company narrative, positioning, proof points, product explanations, previous deck patterns, common objections, and brand rules so future decks improve instead of restarting.

FAQ

Presentation Design — Questions Answered

Do you write the deck or only design it?

MyDesigner can sharpen structure and clarity around content you provide. Full copywriting or strategy depth depends on the scope.

Can you redesign an existing pitch deck?

Yes. This is one of the strongest use cases: making existing thinking sharper, more visual, and easier to present.

Can you make reusable templates?

Yes. Reusable slide patterns can be stored in Client Memory so future sales and founder communication gets faster.

Which formats do you deliver?

We can deliver PowerPoint, Keynote, Google Slides, or Figma files depending on how your team presents and edits.
