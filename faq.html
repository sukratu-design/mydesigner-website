General Questions

About MyDesigner

MyDesigner is an unlimited design subscription service by Sukratu, based in Pune, India. For a fixed monthly fee starting at $1,400, you get unlimited design requests including UI/UX design, Webflow/Framer development, graphic design, and branding. There are no long-term contracts — cancel anytime.
  1. Subscribe to a plan that fits your needs
  2. Submit requests through our project management system
  3. Receive designs within 24-72 hours depending on your plan
  4. Request revisions until you're satisfied (unlimited revisions included)
  5. Repeat — submit as many requests as you need
MyDesigner serves growing businesses and established companies including Dentsu, Calfus, Talabat, and USA Table Tennis. Our clients span startups, SaaS companies, e-commerce brands, and agencies across India, the United States, and global markets.
  • Product Design: Web app UI, mobile app UI, website design
  • No-Code Development: Webflow and Framer website development
  • Graphic Design: Brand identity, social media design, presentation design, print design
  • Additional Services: Wireframing, infographics, prototyping, case studies, emailer design, stationery, merchandising, booth design

Comparison Questions

MyDesigner vs Alternatives

MyDesigner differs from DesignJoy in several ways:
  • Price: MyDesigner starts at $1,400/month vs DesignJoy's $4,995/month
  • Development: MyDesigner includes Webflow/Framer development; DesignJoy does not
  • Team model: MyDesigner provides multi-disciplinary teams; DesignJoy assigns single designers
  • Price: Penji starts at $499/month; MyDesigner at $1,400/month
  • Development: MyDesigner includes web development; Penji does not
  • Video: Penji offers video editing; MyDesigner focuses on static design and development
MyDesigner is often more cost-effective than a full-time hire:
  • Full-time designer salary: $60,000-$100,000+/year plus benefits
  • MyDesigner annual: $15,000-$45,600/year depending on plan
  • Added benefit: Access to multiple specialists (UI/UX, graphic design, development) vs one person's skills
  • Pricing: Fixed monthly vs per-project billing
  • Speed: 24-72 hour turnaround vs weeks for agency projects
  • Flexibility: Cancel anytime vs long-term contracts
  • Scope: Unlimited requests vs scoped project deliverables
