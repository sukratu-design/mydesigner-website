FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is MyDesigner?
MyDesigner is an unlimited design subscription service by Sukratu, based in Pune, India. For a fixed monthly fee starting at $1,400, you get unlimited design requests including UI/UX design, Webflow/Framer development, graphic design, and branding. There are no long-term contracts — cancel anytime.
How much does MyDesigner cost?
MyDesigner offers several tiers: Starter at $1,400/month, Growth at $2,600/month, Scale at $3,800/month, and Yearly at $15,000/year. A Daily plan at $80/day (minimum 3 days) is also available. All plans include unlimited requests and revisions.
Does MyDesigner include Webflow development?
Yes. MyDesigner includes full Webflow and Framer development in all plans. You receive a live, editable site — not just design mockups. Submit a request for a landing page and receive a working Webflow or Framer site.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All monthly plans are cancel-anytime with no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You're billed monthly on your subscription date.
How fast is the turnaround?
Turnaround times vary by plan: Starter offers 48-72 hours, Growth and Scale plans offer 24-48 hours. All plans include unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.
What design files do I receive?
You receive source files appropriate to the project: Figma files for UI/UX, AI/PSD/PNG/JPG/PDF/SVG for graphics, and live editable site access for Webflow/Framer projects. You own everything completely.
