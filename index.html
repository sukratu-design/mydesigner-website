FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is MyDesigner?
MyDesigner is an AI-native creative team for growth by Sukratu. We help founders and growing teams ship high-converting websites, founder content, brand systems, campaign assets, and Webflow/Framer builds with AI-speed production and human creative direction.
How much does MyDesigner cost?
Growth design subscriptions start at $1,400/month. Focused creative sprints start at $2,500, conversion website sprints are scoped after a call, and deeper Creative Growth Partner retainers are scoped around your goals.
Does MyDesigner include Webflow development?
Yes. MyDesigner includes full Webflow and Framer development in all plans. You receive a live, editable site — not just design mockups. Submit a request for a landing page and receive a working Webflow or Framer site.
Do we have to start with ongoing support?
Yes. Monthly operating rhythms are flexible, and focused sprints can be scoped as defined projects when ongoing support is not the right fit.
How fast is the turnaround?
Turnaround depends on scope and operating rhythm. Focused assets can move quickly, while larger websites, product UI, and brand systems are planned around the quality bar and review cycle.
Is MyDesigner worth it?
MyDesigner is worth it for companies that need consistent design output without a full-time hire. A full-time designer costs $60,000–$100,000+/year; MyDesigner starts at $1,400/month and gives you a multi-disciplinary team covering UI/UX, graphic design, and Webflow/Framer development. Clients like Dentsu and USA Table Tennis use it for ongoing design needs a single hire couldn't cover.
