FAQ

Common questions about the process

What info do I need to start?

To get started, provide a brief describing your project, any brand assets (logos, colors, fonts), and reference examples of designs you like. The more detail you share, the faster and more accurately we can deliver. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we'll guide you through what's needed.

How do I communicate with the team?

All communication happens through our project management system. Submit requests, leave feedback, request revisions, and track progress — all in one place. No scattered emails or Slack threads. Everything stays organized and transparent.

Can I change my plan?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. No penalties or fees for switching. Start with the Starter plan and upgrade to Growth or Scale as your needs grow.

What if I'm not satisfied?

All plans include unlimited revisions. Request changes until you're 100% satisfied — there is no cap on the number of revision rounds. If the service isn't right for you, cancel anytime. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
