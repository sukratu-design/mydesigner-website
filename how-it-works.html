Why the old way breaks

The old way treats creative work like isolated tasks.

Most design workflows start with a request and end with a file. That works for one-off tasks, but it breaks when your company needs to move fast across websites, campaigns, content, and brand systems.

The problem is not just production speed. It is context. If every new asset starts from scratch, your team keeps re-explaining the same brand rules, reviewing the same mistakes, and fixing the same inconsistencies.

MyDesigner works differently. Every project adds to a living creative system, so future work gets faster, sharper, and more consistent.

FAQ

Common questions about the workflow

Is MyDesigner an AI tool or a creative team?
MyDesigner is a creative team that uses AI-powered workflows. AI helps us explore, produce, and adapt faster. Human creative direction controls strategy, taste, brand fit, and final quality.
Will the work look generic if AI is involved?
No. Client Memory captures your brand rules, approved examples, rejected directions, and preferences so AI-assisted work stays guided by your actual context.
What is Client Memory?
Client Memory is a living creative system for your brand: rules, messaging, templates, examples, prompt patterns, feedback history, and learnings that help every new project start with context.
Can I still submit normal design requests?
Yes. You can request websites, landing pages, decks, social creatives, UI screens, brand assets, and campaign materials. The difference is that requests are handled within a broader creative system.
How fast do you deliver?
Timelines depend on scope and plan. Smaller creative requests can move quickly, while websites, landing pages, and brand systems follow a focused sprint or production cycle.
