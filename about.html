02 Why We Exist

Same particles. Different arrangement.

AI tools made production fast. They also made production generic. The work can arrive instantly and still feel like it came from nowhere.

MyDesigner combines AI-speed execution with human creative direction and taste. AI gives speed. Human direction gives shape.

Thesis — speed is useful only when judgment has the controls.

03 How We Operate

One revolution. Five fixed moves.

Not a project-scoped agency handoff. An ongoing creative operating rhythm that learns the brand and gets faster.

Diagnose

Start with the business problem before choosing the artifact.

Build Client Memory

Capture the context that should make the next request sharper than the last.

Create

Use AI-speed production under human creative direction and taste.

Ship

Deliver approved final work and source files in usable formats.

Learn

Feed approved, rejected, and performance learnings back into the next orbit.

04 Client Memory

The work should remember you.

Core thesis

AI tools made production fast but also generic. MyDesigner combines AI-speed execution with human creative direction and taste.

AI gives speed. Human direction gives shape.
