Key Differences

Development Included (MyDesigner Advantage)

MyDesigner includes Webflow and Framer development in all plans. Design a landing page and get it built — no separate contractor needed.

Example workflow:

  • Penji: Design mockup → Find developer → Handoff → Revisions → Launch
  • MyDesigner: Design mockup → Same team builds it → Launch

Video Editing (Penji Advantage)

Penji's Pro and Team plans include video editing for social media content, ads, and presentations. MyDesigner focuses on static design and web development.

Price Point

Penji starts lower at $499/month but with limitations (1 project at a time, basic design only). MyDesigner's $1,400 entry includes more comprehensive services and faster turnaround.

Team Structure

  • Penji: Assigns designers from a pool; you may work with different designers
  • MyDesigner: Dedicated team familiar with your brand (especially on Growth/Scale plans)

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyDesigner more expensive than Penji?

Yes, MyDesigner starts at $1,400/month vs Penji's $499/month. However, MyDesigner includes web development that would cost extra with Penji. For design + development needs, MyDesigner can be more cost-effective.

Which has faster turnaround?

Both offer 24-48 hour turnaround for standard requests. MyDesigner's Starter plan quotes 48-72 hours; Growth plan offers 24-48 hours.

Can I use both services?

Some teams use Penji for high-volume social content and MyDesigner for product design and web development. The services can complement each other.

Which is better for startups?

It depends on your stage. Pre-revenue/bootstrapped: Penji at $499/month is a budget-friendly starting point. Funded/revenue-generating: MyDesigner at $1,400/month is the stronger choice for the development inclusion.

Make Your Decision

Penji excels at affordable, high-volume graphic design with video editing capabilities. MyDesigner is the better choice when you need design + web development under one roof, or prefer dedicated team relationships.
