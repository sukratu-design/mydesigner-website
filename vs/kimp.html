FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kimp cheaper than MyDesigner?

For graphics only, yes ($599 vs $1,400). But if you need web development, Kimp + developer often exceeds MyDesigner's all-in price.

Does Kimp build websites?

No. Kimp designs website mockups but doesn't develop them. You'd need to hire a separate Webflow/Framer developer.

Which has faster turnaround?

Kimp advertises 24-hour turnaround. MyDesigner ranges from 24-72 hours, with Growth/Scale plans offering faster response.

Can I get video editing from MyDesigner?

No. MyDesigner focuses on static design and web development. For video, Kimp or another service is needed.

Which is better for startups?

Bootstrapped, content-focused startups should consider Kimp ($599-$1,198/month). Funded startups building product should consider MyDesigner ($1,400-$3,800/month).
