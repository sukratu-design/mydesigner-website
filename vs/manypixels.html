FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ManyPixels cheaper than MyDesigner?

Yes, ManyPixels starts at $549/month vs MyDesigner's $1,400/month. However, if you need web development, hiring a separate developer often costs more than the price difference.

Which service has better quality?

Both deliver professional-quality work. ManyPixels has a larger global team; MyDesigner offers more personalized, dedicated team relationships especially on higher plans.

Can I get Webflow sites from ManyPixels?

ManyPixels designs Webflow-ready mockups but doesn't develop them. You'd need a separate Webflow developer. MyDesigner designs AND builds the site.

Which is better for ongoing product design?

MyDesigner's dedicated team model works better for product design where understanding context matters. ManyPixels is optimized for higher-volume, standalone requests.
