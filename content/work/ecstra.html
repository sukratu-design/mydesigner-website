The Challenge

Ecstra operates in the home improvement sector, specialising in waterproofing and repair solutions. They had a clear brand identity and an approved design — what they needed was a development partner who could bring it to life on the web with precision, speed, and the flexibility to grow.

The challenge was twofold: faithfully implementing a detailed design without losing any of its visual intent, and building a structure that Ecstra's team could manage independently without technical help day-to-day. For a company in a trust-led industry, the website also needed to project competence and reliability from the first scroll.

Our Approach

We built the site entirely in Webflow, taking full advantage of its CMS and visual development capabilities. Every section was developed to match the design pixel-accurately — from navigation behaviour to hover states, call-to-action placement, and responsive breakpoints. Interactions and transitions were kept purposeful: smooth enough to feel premium, never so heavy they slowed the page.

Navigation and CTAs were optimised for clarity at every stage of the user journey, ensuring visitors could find what they needed — whether they were homeowners looking for a solution or trade professionals evaluating a supplier.

The Result

Ecstra launched with a fast, intuitive website that effectively bridges their offline expertise and digital presence. The Webflow CMS means their team can update content, add projects, and manage the site independently. The result is a platform that reinforces their industry leadership and gives them a credible, conversion-ready digital home.
