07 Questions

Asked & answered.

What exactly is MyDesigner? +

An AI-native creative studio by Sukratu. We help founders and growing teams ship high-converting websites, founder content, AI-ready brand systems, campaign assets, and Webflow/Framer builds — with AI-speed production and human creative direction.

Why not just use AI tools ourselves? +

You should — for drafts. But AI gives you options, not direction. Growth teams still need taste, prioritization, brand memory, and production discipline. That judgment layer is what you're actually buying.

How fast is the turnaround? +

Most focused assets ship in 24–72 hours. Larger websites, product UI, and brand systems are planned around the quality bar and review cycle, with timelines agreed up front.

Is Webflow / Framer development included? +

Yes, in all plans. You receive a live, editable site — not just design mockups.

Why is pricing per week? +

Because with AI-speed production, a week is what a month used to be. Agencies sell months because their process needs them; ours doesn't. Weekly pricing keeps the commitment small and the accountability high — every week has to ship something. Ongoing plans are still billed monthly to keep admin simple.

Do we have to commit to ongoing weeks? +

No. Start with a single Ship Week — one defined outcome, shipped in five days. Continue into Growth Weeks only if that first week earns it.
